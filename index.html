A cortado is a balanced coffee drink made with equal parts espresso and steamed milk, offering a smooth, rich flavor. Its name means "cut" in Spanish, referring to how the milk softens the espresso’s bold intensity without diluting it.


A latte is a creamy, comforting coffee drink made with a shot of espresso and a generous amount of steamed milk, topped with a light layer of foam. It’s smooth, mellow, and perfect for those who enjoy a softer, milk-forward coffee experience.


An espresso is a bold and concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans under high pressure. It’s rich, intense, and serves as the foundation for many popular coffee drinks.


A mocha is a delicious blend of espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate, combining the boldness of coffee with the sweetness of cocoa. Topped with whipped cream or foam, it’s a rich and indulgent treat perfect for chocolate and coffee lovers alike.
